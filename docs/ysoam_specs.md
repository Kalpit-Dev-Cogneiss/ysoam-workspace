## What is YSOAM?

YSOAM is a single platform that brings together everything you need to run a profitable fleet:
- Real-time GPS tracking of your vehicles
- Trip management and dispatch
- Fuel, maintenance, and inventory tracking
- Automatic billing and invoicing
- **Most importantly: See exactly which trips make money and which lose money**

No more juggling 5 different spreadsheets or systems. Everything in one place.

## The Problem YSOAM Solves

**Current Situation (Without YSOAM):**
- ❌ Don't know which routes are profitable
- ❌ Manual data entry in multiple systems (GPS tracking app, Excel for billing, another app for fuel)

## Core Features (What You Get)

### 1. Real-Time GPS Tracking
- See all vehicles on a live map
- Know exact location, speed, idle time
- Alerts if vehicles go offline
- Historical route playback (see where vehicles have been)

### 2. Trip Management
- Create trips and auto-assign to best driver/vehicle
- Track trip from pickup → delivery → completion
- Monitor status in real-time
- See if trip is on schedule or delayed

### 3. Profitability Dashboard (THE HERO FEATURE)
- **Trip Profitability:** See profit for every trip
  - Revenue from customer
  - Minus: fuel cost, driver advance, maintenance estimate
  - = Net profit and margin %
- **Vehicle Profitability:** See which vehicles are most profitable
  - Total trips completed
  - Average profit per trip
  - How much the vehicle is being used
- **Route Profitability:** See which routes make the most money
  - Best routes (keep doing these)
  - Problem routes (optimize or drop)
  - Recommended routes (AI suggestions)

**Example:**
- Trip Mumbai → Pune
  - Revenue: ₹4,500
  - Fuel cost: ₹2,100
  - Driver advance: ₹500
  - Maintenance: ₹450
  - **Profit: ₹1,450 (32% margin)** ✓ Good trip

### 4. Driver Management
- Track all drivers in system
- See driver assignment and performance
- Monitor driver behavior (speeding, harsh braking, idle time)
- Pay drivers advances from YSOAM

### 5. Vehicle Management
- Complete database of all vehicles
- Track insurance, permits, fitness certificates (with expiry alerts)
- Monitor fuel, maintenance history per vehicle
- See vehicle utilization and downtime

### 6. Fuel Management
- Log fuel entries for every refuel
- Automatically calculate fuel consumption (km driven / liters used)
- Detect fuel theft (if consumption abnormal)
- See fuel trends and costs

### 7. Maintenance & Breakdown Tracking
- Log breakdown events (vehicle broken down, when, where)
- Track repair costs and history
- Get maintenance alerts before problems happen
- Know downtime per vehicle and its cost

### 8. Automatic Billing
- Create invoices automatically from trips
- Add GST automatically
- Send invoices to customers
- Track who paid and who owes money

### 9. Customer Ledger
- See total money owed by each customer
- Track payment history
- Identify customers who owe you money
- Forecast cash flow

### 10. Inventory Management
- Track spare parts, tyres, lubricants
- Low stock alerts (reorder before you run out)
- See what's being used on which vehicles

### 11. Document Management
- Store all documents (insurance, permits, fitness, licenses) in one place
- Get alerts when documents are about to expire
- No more lost documents

### 12. Battery Management
- Monitor battery health for electric vehicles
- Get alerts for low battery
- Track battery lifespan and replacement costs

### 13. Tyre Tracking
- Know which tyres are on which vehicles
- Track tyre lifespan
- Monitor tyre replacement costs
- Optimize retreading decisions

### 14. Reports & Analytics
- Daily reports on vehicle performance
- Fuel consumption reports
- Profitability trends over time
- Export reports to PDF/Excel for presentations

### 15. Mobile-Friendly
- Use on phone, tablet, or computer
- Responsive design (works on any device)
- Drivers can log fuel, trips via mobile

### 16. Multi-User Access
- Different permissions for different people
  - Admin: Full access
  - Manager: See all vehicles and trips
  - Operator: Manage assigned vehicles only
  - Driver: Log trips and fuel

### 17. Cloud-Based & Secure
- Access from anywhere (office, home, on-the-road)
- Automatic backup (never lose data)
- Secure login (password + 2-factor authentication)
- Encrypted data transfer

### 18. Future-Ready Features
- Integration with e-way bills and GST (coming)
- AI recommendations (coming)
- Advanced geofencing (coming)
- Native mobile apps for Android/iPhone (coming)

### 19. Scalability
- Works with 10 vehicles today
- Scales to 100+ vehicles tomorrow
- No system changes needed when you grow

## How YSOAM Works (User Journey)

### Day 1: Manager Logs In
1. Open ysoam.com
2. See live dashboard with all vehicles on map
3. See today's trips and their status
4. See today's profit so far

### Day 2: Create a Trip
1. Customer calls for a trip: Mumbai → Pune
2. Manager creates trip in YSOAM
3. System shows: "This route averages ₹2,300 profit"
4. Assign best driver (based on history)
5. Trip is live on map

### Day 3: Trip Completes
1. Driver logs arrival/completion
2. Fuel is logged
3. System auto-calculates: Profit ₹1,450
4. Invoice auto-created and sent to customer

### Week 1: Review Profitability
1. Manager opens Profitability Dashboard
2. Sees: "Best route this week: Mumbai → Delhi (₹2,800 avg profit)"
3. Sees: "Problem route: Pune → Bangalore (₹850 avg profit)"
4. Decision: Focus on Delhi routes next week

### Month 1: Report for Stakeholders
1. Export profitability report
2. Share with owners/investors
3. Show which vehicles/drivers are most profitable
4. Plan next month based on data
