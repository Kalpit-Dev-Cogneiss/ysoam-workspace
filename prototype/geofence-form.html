Adding a Location

Use the address search below, or input the latitude and longitude of the location. You can also draw a geofence directly on the map after address or coordinates are input.

The geofence radius is used to determine the location entries that are associated to this geofence. You can use this geofence to send an alert when a location entry is here.

Polygon geofences are drawn on the map. Radius does not apply.

You can draw a geofence directly on the map by clicking the circle or polygon icon.

Click and drag on the map to draw a circular geofence.

Click to add corners. Click the first point or double-click to finish.
